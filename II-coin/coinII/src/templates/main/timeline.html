II  Timeline

Initiative Q is still in its early days, and there is a long way to go before all your Qs could be used at around one USD per Q.

Initiative Q's roadmap is based on a series of steps towards widespread use of the Q currency. Each step is designed such that participants will view its fulfillment as a rational decision, given completion of the previous steps and the Q incentives they receive.

Still, this roadmap carries many risks and challenges, and there is no guarantee that any of these steps will be successfully completed
